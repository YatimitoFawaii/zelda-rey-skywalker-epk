About Zelda

Zelda Rey Skywalker has been playing music her whole life and made it full-time work in 2024. She is proficient on piano/keyboards, guitar, and bass, with additional experience on accordion and drums.

Her work spans live performance, music production, live audio technologies, and multimedia. She is a graduate of the UCLA School of Theater, Film and Television, lived in Las Vegas for six years, and is now based again in Southern California.
